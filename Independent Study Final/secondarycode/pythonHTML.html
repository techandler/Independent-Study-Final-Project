Python

I started out my independent study by learning python. Once I learned some

basic python code, I went on to learn a library called pygame which

allowed me to do more interesting things in python. I then proceeded to make

the game pong. At first, it was tough finding out all of the different codes

required in order to create a game that worked well. Once I finished pong,

I started making a new game, this time, with all of the knowledge I gained

from making pong, it was much easier. I ended up making a game in which the

player controls an orange car on the bottom of the screen, their goal is

to dodge oncoming cars and not hit the sidewalk. If you would like to play

one of these games, they are available in the "Pong" and "CarGame" folders

inside the "Independent Study Final" folder.

(For the choice of single player or two player pong, pick "PongExperiments.py")
